# Monthly MIS Commentary - Sample Output

## 1. Executive Summary

Revenue for the current month stands at Rs. 50.0 lakh, up 11.1% from the previous month (Rs. 45.0 lakh) and up 4.2% from the same month last year (Rs. 48.0 lakh). However, revenue is 3.8% below budget (Rs. 52.0 lakh). Gross margin has remained stable at 40%. Operating profit is Rs. 8.0 lakh, up from Rs. 7.0 lakh in the prior month but 15.8% below budget. Cash position has declined to Rs. 12.0 lakh from Rs. 14.0 lakh in the previous month, which requires attention.

## 2. Revenue Movement

- Current month: Rs. 50.0 lakh
- Previous month: Rs. 45.0 lakh (increase of Rs. 5.0 lakh, +11.1%)
- Same month last year: Rs. 48.0 lakh (increase of Rs. 2.0 lakh, +4.2%)
- Budget: Rs. 52.0 lakh (shortfall of Rs. 2.0 lakh, -3.8%)

Revenue growth vs prior month is encouraging, driven by higher sales volumes. However, the shortfall against budget should be investigated.

## 3. Margin Movement

- Gross margin %: 40% (current) vs 40% (previous) vs 39.6% (last year) vs 40.4% (budget)
- Gross margin % is stable and within acceptable range.
- No significant flag here.

## 4. Expense Movement

- Operating expenses: Rs. 12.0 lakh (current) vs Rs. 11.0 lakh (previous), an increase of Rs. 1.0 lakh (+9.1%).
- Operating expenses vs budget: Rs. 12.0 lakh actual vs Rs. 11.5 lakh budget, an overspend of Rs. 0.5 lakh (+4.3%).
- This increase in expenses needs review to check whether it is one-time or recurring.

## 5. Working Capital Notes

- Receivables over 60 days: Rs. 8.5 lakh (current) vs Rs. 7.0 lakh (previous), an increase of Rs. 1.5 lakh (+21.4%).
- This is a FLAG. Collection efforts need to be strengthened.
- Payables outstanding: Rs. 4.5 lakh (current) vs Rs. 4.2 lakh (previous), a slight increase.

## 6. Cash-Flow Notes

- Cash at bank: Rs. 12.0 lakh (current) vs Rs. 14.0 lakh (previous), a decline of Rs. 2.0 lakh (-14.3%).
- This is a FLAG. Cash position has dropped below Rs. 15.0 lakh. Review collection timeline and payment schedules.

## 7. Questions for Management

1. Why is revenue below budget despite growth over prior month?
2. What is driving the increase in operating expenses?
3. What is the plan to reduce receivables over 60 days?
4. What steps are being taken to improve cash position?
