# Review Checklist

Before finalizing the MIS commentary, check:

- Are all numbers accurate?
- Are all required inputs present?
- Does commentary match the source data?
- Are unusual changes clearly flagged?
- Are revenue, margin, expense, receivables, payables, and cash comments correct?
- Is the language professional and concise?
- Is anything missing that the client would expect?
- Has a human reviewed and approved the draft?

Final rule:
- If anything is unclear, edit it before sending.
- AI output is only a draft.
