# Use Cases

This agent is designed for the following use cases in finance and accounting.

## 1. Monthly MIS Commentary for CFO Teams

- Input: Monthly P&L, balance sheet, aging reports, and bank summary.
- Output: Draft commentary explaining month-on-month movements, flagged exceptions, and questions for management.
- Value: Cuts manual commentary writing time by 60-70% and ensures consistent reporting format.

## 2. Monthly MIS for Virtual CFO Firms

- Input: Client financial data uploaded in CSV or extracted from ERP.
- Output: Standardized commentary with flags, executive summary, and cash-flow notes.
- Value: Enables virtual CFOs to serve more clients with less manual effort per client.

## 3. Accounting Firm Internal Draft Support

- Input: Client trial balance and key schedules.
- Output: First-draft commentary for review by the engagement partner.
- Value: Reduces partner review burden and improves consistency across client engagements.

## 4. SME Finance Review by Founder or Controller

- Input: Basic monthly reports from accountant.
- Output: Plain-language explanation of what changed, what is risky, and what questions to ask.
- Value: Makes financial data understandable for non-CFO founders who need visibility.

## 5. Audit Pre-Close Review

- Input: Preliminary month-end data before books are closed.
- Output: Early flags on unusual movements that need investigation before final close.
- Value: Catches issues before final numbers are locked.

## Important Notes

- This agent produces a draft only. All outputs must be reviewed by a qualified finance professional.
- This agent does not give tax, legal, or statutory advice.
- This agent does not post entries, approve payments, or take any action in financial systems.
- Accuracy depends on the quality of input data provided by the user.

## Who Should Not Use This

- Anyone who expects fully automated final reports without human review.
- Anyone who needs statutory, regulatory, or tax filings.
- Anyone who needs this agent to make financial decisions automatically.
